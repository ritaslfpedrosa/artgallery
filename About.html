ABOUT

Art Gallery of Coimbra is a young and dynamic Coimbra-based art gallery. We want to promote our emerging or mid-career artists in major international art fairs throughout Asia, Austria, Belgium, Germany, London, the Netherlands, New York, Switzerland ... We want to offer contemporary art in a relaxed and approachable atmosphere, providing a fun and exciting art selection for you to gain access, increase your knowledge, fall in love and... buy art! Our Art Gallery chooses to represent art and artists who do not fit into any particular current trend or fashion, but who are exploring various media, techniques or ideas that are timeless. We believe art is a gift to be shared, given and received with appreciation and respect. Share your passion, spread our art.
